Flagle

Rules:

Country mode (Daily): You have 8 tries to guess the correct flag in as least tries as possible. You can view each country name by clicking on the country menu at the bottom of the screen, or the country names will show up on the sides of the flag. After you guess, the continent to country is in will appear under the image of the flag. The screen will flash red if your guess is in the wrong continent, yellow if its in the right contenent, and green if it is correct. If you type in hint you will get the first letter of the country. A hint counts as a guess. There is no daily state mode. You can only get a hint after 3 guesses. Daily mode restarts at 12 AM UCT everyday so come back every day to try and guess the flag in as least tries as possible.

Country and State Mode (Endless): You have 8 tries to guess the country or state. If you guess correctly, it will count on your streak. If your guess is incorrect, your streak is lost. You can view each country / state name by clicking on the menu at the bottom of the screen (if enabled in the settings), or the country names will show up on the sides of the flag. Hints work the same way for endless, but after 1 guess you can get a hint.

If you guess on the correct continent, a blue checkmark will apppear, and if you guess correctly, there will be a green checkmark, and incorrectly, a red X.

Want a challenge? Try hard mode by clicking the skeleton at the top of the screen!
